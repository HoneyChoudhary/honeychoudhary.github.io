Hi, I'm Honey Choudhary
and I am a Graphic Designer. a Social Media Manager. an Illustrator. an Artist. a Tech Enthusiast. a Free Thinker.

I am Designer and Social Media Manager living in Mumbai, India, passionate about Design, Graphic Development, and Technology in general. I'm a designer on a variety of technical and creative projects including adverts, newsletters, banners, and brouchers. I love solving problems with design, and making great ideas happen.

What do I do ?

Graphic Designing

I professionally develop high quality graphical assets and designs using Adobe Photoshop, Coral Draw, InDesign and Illustrator. Using these tools I develop a design flow mechanism for various brands and design projects.

Motion Graphics

I believe in well organized graphical assets and pixel perfection. In addition to creating static designs, I also have experience in creating motion graphics and GIF images.

The World

Besides all this I take any creative as a challenge and ensure I give my best. I like dancing, singing and love to play around with creative stuff.
